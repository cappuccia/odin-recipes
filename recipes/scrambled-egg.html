Scrambled Egg

Here's How To Make a Scrambled Egg

The secret to making scrambled eggs is all about patience...and the proper pan. Here's a quick step-by-step guide to show you how to make scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Chop Onion and Garlic into very small cubes
  3. Break the eggs on a bowl
  4. Put Onion and Garlic into bowl
  5. Put Salt and Pepper iinto bowl
  6. Whisk eggs thoroughly
  7. Heat the pan
  8. Pour Cooking oil into pan
  9. Pour whisked eggs onto boiling oil
  10. Stir
  11. Remove pan from heat and continue to stir
  12. Serve!
